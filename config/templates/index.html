SECURITY



Personal Safety

Although a safe bike ride also depends on external conditions, the essential thing is to be protected. Safety for cyclists starts with the cyclist, so personal safety is a priority. No matter what type of cycling you practice, these recommendations apply before, during and after the tour.

  • – Plan the route: Planning your route is crucial, whether in city or countryside, considering mileage and height to ensure a smooth and efficient journey
  • – Have a map: Ensure to have a map of your destination, whether printed or electronic, and ensure your cell phone has sufficient battery life and WiFi.
  • – Check the conditions of the bicycle: Check the brakes, make sure that the tires are inflated and do not have any type of hole. It is recommended to do a general review every 6 months.
  • – Bring a tool and first aid kit: Whether you need to do some emergency repair, or have a minor accident along the way. It is always important to take these two kits with us when cycling.
